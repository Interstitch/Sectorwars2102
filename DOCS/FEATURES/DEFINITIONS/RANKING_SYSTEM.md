# Military Ranking System
**SectorWars 2102 - Galactic Fleet Command Ranks**

**Last Updated**: 2025-11-16
**Version**: 1.0
**Purpose**: Achievement-based military progression system

---

## Overview

The Galactic Fleet Command maintains a standardized ranking system for all space pilots and commanders operating in the year 2102. Ranks are earned through demonstrated combat prowess, economic achievement, territorial control, and contributions to galactic civilization.

**Rank Display**: Your military rank is displayed prominently on your player profile, in leaderboards, and appears in team rosters. Other players can see your rank, making it a badge of honor and achievement.

**Progression**: Ranks are permanent once earned and cannot be lost, though players can continue advancing through higher ranks based on cumulative achievements.

---

## Ranking Structure

### Enlisted Ranks (Entry Level)

#### **Recruit**
- **Requirements**: New player, first login completed
- **Insignia**: Single silver bar
- **Description**: Fresh pilot with basic space certification. Just received your first ship and ready to learn the basics of interstellar commerce and navigation.
- **Benefits**: Access to basic training materials and ARIA's Educational Mode

#### **Spacer**
- **Requirements**:
  - 10,000 credits earned
  - 50 sectors visited
  - 25 successful trades
- **Insignia**: Double silver bars
- **Description**: Competent navigator who understands basic trading routes and sector navigation. Proven ability to earn credits through commerce.
- **Benefits**: +2% trading bonus at all ports

#### **Corporal**
- **Requirements**:
  - 50,000 credits earned
  - 100 sectors visited
  - 100 successful trades
  - First combat victory
- **Insignia**: Three silver bars
- **Description**: Experienced trader who has engaged in combat and survived. Demonstrates both economic savvy and tactical awareness.
- **Benefits**: +3% trading bonus, access to basic military outposts

---

### Non-Commissioned Officer Ranks (Intermediate)

#### **Sergeant**
- **Requirements**:
  - 100,000 credits earned
  - 200 sectors visited
  - 250 successful trades
  - 10 combat victories
  - Owns 1 planet or has joined a team
- **Insignia**: Bronze chevron with one rocker
- **Description**: Proven leader in both commerce and combat. Demonstrates territorial ambitions and collaborative teamwork.
- **Benefits**: +5% trading bonus, can lead small trading convoys

#### **Staff Sergeant**
- **Requirements**:
  - 500,000 credits earned
  - 500 sectors visited
  - 500 successful trades
  - 25 combat victories
  - Owns 2+ planets or leads a team
  - Completed 5 faction missions
- **Insignia**: Bronze chevron with two rockers
- **Description**: Distinguished combat veteran and successful entrepreneur. Recognized by factions for reliable mission completion.
- **Benefits**: +6% trading bonus, +5% faction reputation gain

#### **Master Sergeant**
- **Requirements**:
  - 1,000,000 credits earned
  - 1,000 sectors visited
  - 1,000 successful trades
  - 50 combat victories
  - Owns 3+ planets or manages an active team
  - Completed 15 faction missions
  - 1 quantum trade executed
- **Insignia**: Bronze chevron with three rockers and star
- **Description**: Elite non-commissioned operator with mastery of advanced trading techniques including quantum mechanics. Commands respect throughout the galaxy.
- **Benefits**: +7% trading bonus, +10% faction reputation gain, priority docking at major ports

---

### Warrant Officer Ranks (Specialist)

#### **Warrant Officer**
- **Requirements**:
  - 2,500,000 credits earned
  - 1,500 sectors visited
  - 100 combat victories
  - Owns 5+ planets
  - Created 1 warp tunnel
  - Successfully led a team fleet battle
- **Insignia**: Gold bar with silver diamond
- **Description**: Technical specialist and tactical expert. Has demonstrated ability to construct infrastructure and lead coordinated military operations.
- **Benefits**: +8% trading bonus, can construct advanced facilities

#### **Chief Warrant Officer**
- **Requirements**:
  - 5,000,000 credits earned
  - 2,000 sectors visited
  - 200 combat victories
  - Owns 8+ planets
  - Created 3+ warp tunnels
  - Led 5+ fleet battles
  - Owns 1 port
- **Insignia**: Gold bar with gold diamond
- **Description**: Master technician and infrastructure specialist. Controls significant commercial and military infrastructure across multiple sectors.
- **Benefits**: +10% trading bonus, reduced warp tunnel construction costs

---

### Commissioned Officer Ranks (Leadership)

#### **Ensign**
- **Requirements**:
  - 10,000,000 credits earned
  - 300 combat victories
  - Owns 10+ planets with advanced infrastructure
  - Team leader with 3+ active members
  - Controls 1+ sector completely
  - Completed 50 faction missions (Friendly+ reputation)
- **Insignia**: Gold bar
- **Description**: Junior commissioned officer demonstrating exceptional leadership. Controls substantial territory and commands loyal team members.
- **Benefits**: +12% trading bonus, can negotiate regional governance roles

#### **Lieutenant**
- **Requirements**:
  - 25,000,000 credits earned
  - 500 combat victories
  - Owns 12+ planets
  - Led team to top 10 in any regional leaderboard
  - Controls 3+ sectors
  - Executed 25+ quantum trades
  - Allied status with 2+ factions
- **Insignia**: Two gold bars
- **Description**: Accomplished fleet commander with significant regional influence. Proven mastery of quantum trading and diplomatic relations.
- **Benefits**: +15% trading bonus, +15% faction reputation gain, diplomatic immunity in allied territories

#### **Commander**
- **Requirements**:
  - 50,000,000 credits earned
  - 1,000 combat victories
  - Owns 15+ planets across multiple regions
  - Owns 2+ ports
  - Created own Regional Territory OR top 5 in Central Nexus
  - Successfully defended territory from major attack
  - Allied status with 5+ factions
- **Insignia**: Three gold bars
- **Description**: Elite commander of vast resources and influence. Rules extensive territory and commands significant military and economic power.
- **Benefits**: +18% trading bonus, reduced regional taxes, preferential treatment galaxy-wide

#### **Captain**
- **Requirements**:
  - 100,000,000 credits earned
  - 2,500 combat victories
  - Owns 20+ planets
  - Owns 5+ ports
  - Successfully governs a Regional Territory OR top 3 in Central Nexus
  - Led alliance of 3+ teams
  - Reputation maxed (Allied) with 10+ factions
  - Owns a Warp Jumper ship
- **Insignia**: Gold eagle
- **Description**: Legendary captain commanding fleets across the galaxy. Name is known throughout civilized space as both trader and warrior.
- **Benefits**: +20% trading bonus, significant diplomatic influence, can negotiate special trade agreements

## Special Commendations & Medals

Beyond ranks, players can earn special medals for extraordinary achievements:

### Combat Medals
- **Bronze Star**: 100 combat victories
- **Silver Star**: 1,000 combat victories
- **Quantum Cross**: 10,000 combat victories
- **Medal of Valor**: Survived 50+ consecutive combats without defeat

### Economic Medals
- **Trader's Merit**: 1,000 successful trades
- **Merchant Prince**: 10,000 successful trades
- **Economic Titan**: Single trade profit exceeding 1,000,000 credits
- **Market Master**: Correctly predicted 100+ quantum trade outcomes

- **Explorer's Badge**: Discovered 500 unique sectors
- **Pathfinder Medal**: Created 5 new warp tunnels
- **Frontier Spirit**: First to discover 10 rare anomalies

### Diplomatic Medals
- **Ambassador's Star**: Allied reputation with 10 factions
- **Peacekeeper**: Negotiated 25 successful team treaties

## Rank Display & Recognition

**Visual Indicators**:
- Rank insignia displayed next to player name
- Special color coding for Flag Officers (gold glow)
- Animated effects for Fleet Admiral (quantum particle glow)
- Rank badges in chat and messaging
- Leaderboard highlighting

**Social Recognition**:
- Higher ranks receive priority in team recruitment
- NPCs acknowledge rank in dialogue
- Special greetings at ports based on rank
- Rank-based emotes and titles
- Historical records of first players to achieve each rank

## Rank Progression Tips

1. **Balance Multiple Paths**: Don't just focus on combat or trading - ranks reward well-rounded achievement
2. **Team Up**: Many ranks require teamwork and leadership
3. **Explore Widely**: Sector exploration counts toward many ranks
4. **Build Infrastructure**: Planet and port ownership provides permanent assets
5. **Master Quantum Trading**: Advanced ranks heavily reward quantum mastery
6. **Faction Relations**: Maintaining good faction standing opens many doors
7. **Think Long-Term**: The highest ranks are meant to be rare achievements
